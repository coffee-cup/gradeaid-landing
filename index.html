GradeAid

Friendly, Simple, Beautiful Grade Manager

  • Chrome Web Store
  • Github

Add all your classes
with custom identifying colours

Add all your classes you want to keep track of for the semester. Each class will have a customizable colour to easily identify it.

Manage and track Your
grades

For each class add its assignments, midterms, exams, papers, labs, finals, etc. Enter their respective weights and maximum total grade. As you recieve your grades throughout the semester, enter the marks into GradeAid and you will instantly see what your current standing grade in the class is.

Effortlessly know what you need
to reach your target grade

For each mark in a class, GradeAid will automatically let you know what you need to reach your target grade. Simply enter your desired final grade for the class, and you will see what mark you need on the next assignment/exam/paper/etc.

Create schedules for each
of your semesters

Keep your life organized by creating a separate schedule for each semester. Compare each semester to see if your grades are improving.
